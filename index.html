About me

With 10 years as a UI/UX designer and recent experience in web development, my expertise lies at the intersection of design and technology. My strong academic background and international experience set me apart, as does my focus on collaborating with socially engaged institutions, artists, NGOs, educational establishments, and scientific organizations. I'm currently pursuing an interdisciplinary PhD, which combines media arts, new technology, politics, and international relations. A major thrust of my research looks at how interactive forms can be powerful tools for cyber activism. For me, every project is an opportunity to innovate and make a meaningful impact.
